# Acme Robotics — Field Handbook

A small, self-contained demo document for the LAIDocs AI scenarios. Its facts are
deliberately **chained across sections** (the founder is named in one section but
described in another; a product is named here, the factory city there) so that
plain lexical/dense retrieval misses the bridge while GraphRAG recovers it.
Ingest this file in the app, then run the scenarios in `README.md §6`.

## 1. Company

Acme Robotics was founded by Dr. Lena Hoffmann in 2014. The company designs and
builds autonomous warehouse robots. It operates as an independent, privately held
firm.

## 2. Headquarters

Acme Robotics is headquartered in Berlin, Germany. All manufacturing happens at
the Berlin facility; there are no other production sites.

## 3. Leadership

Dr. Lena Hoffmann earned her PhD in mechanical engineering in Munich and was born
in Lyon, France. She continues to serve as the company's chief executive.

## 4. Products

Acme Robotics manufactures the Atlas-7, a warehouse robot for high-throughput
fulfilment centres. The Atlas-7 is the company's only product line.

## 5. People

Marco Ruiz leads the Atlas-7 program. Marco Ruiz reports directly to Dr. Lena
Hoffmann. He joined Acme Robotics in 2019.
